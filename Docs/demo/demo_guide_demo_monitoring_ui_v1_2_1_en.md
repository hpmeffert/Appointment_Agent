# Demo Guide Demo Monitoring UI v1.2.1

Version: v1.2.1
Language: EN

## Demo goal

Show that the platform is now more than a booking demo.

The new story is:
- booking logic
- communication traffic
- operator visibility

## Three story scenarios

1. Booking invitation over RCS
   Show an outbound RCS message, then open the inbound reply.

2. Reminder fallback over SMS
   Show an outbound SMS reminder and explain why SMS still matters.

3. Operator traffic overview
   Open `Message Monitor` and `Reports` to show how communication becomes visible.

## Strong demo line

“This is not just appointment automation anymore. This is the beginning of a messaging conversation platform.”

## Parameters to explain during the demo

- `channel`
  Explains if the traffic uses `RCS` or `SMS`.
- `direction`
  Explains if the message was sent by the platform or by the customer.
- `status`
  Explains if the message was accepted, delivered, received, or failed.
- `journey_id`
  Connects message traffic to one workflow.
- `booking_reference`
  Connects message traffic to one appointment.
